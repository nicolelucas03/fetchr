# Fetchr

**Fetchr** is a modern web application designed to streamline the way you browse, organize, and retrieve images from your Google Drive. Built with Next.js, React, and Supabase, Fetchr empowers content creators, marketers, and social media enthusiasts to quickly find the perfect image for any post or campaign.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Google Drive Integration:** Securely connect your Google account and instantly access your Drive folders.
- **Effortless Browsing:** Navigate your image library with a clean, intuitive interface.
- **Curated for Creators:** Find, preview, and select images tailored for social media and digital content needs.
- **Privacy First:** Your data stays yours—Fetchr uses secure authentication and never stores your images.

## 🚀 Vision

Fetchr aims to become the go-to tool for anyone who needs fast, organized access to their cloud-stored images. Whether you’re planning your next Instagram post or managing a brand’s visual assets, Fetchr helps you stay creative and efficient.
